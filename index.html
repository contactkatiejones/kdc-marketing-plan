KDC's Unfair Advantages — Lead With These Every Time
  • → 30 years in business (since 1995) — no mobile-only competitor or newer shop can match this. It signals trust and stability to fleet managers who need a long-term partner.

  • → J-Pro diagnostic system — professional-grade diagnostics that owner-operators and small fleets can't access elsewhere without going to a dealer.
